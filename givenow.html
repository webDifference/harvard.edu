Make a Gift

Email Google Plus LinkedIn Twitter Facebook

To make a gift online select one of the following options:

  • Make a gift to a School
  • Make a gift to the University
  • Make a gift to a University affiliate
  • Make a gift to the Social Alternative Fund
  • Make a gift of stock
  • Make a gift through your Fidelity or Schwab donor advised fund

To learn more about planned gifts and bequests, contact University Planned Giving.

Many employers sponsor matching gift programs and will match contributions made to Harvard by their employees. To find out if your or your spouse’s company has a matching gift program, use our online search tool.

For assistance, visit the Giving Contacts page or contact Alumni and Development Services:
